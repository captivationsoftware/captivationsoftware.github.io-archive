Get To Know Captivation Software...

Company

Captivation Software was founded in 2014 as a specialty web application studio and has since grown into a comprehensive software solutions provider.

For all inquiries, please contact us or send an email directly to info@captivationsoftware.com or contact us.

Leadership

Dave Barbalato, Owner / Founder

Dave has over 15 years of experience developing custom software using modern web technologies. He has a B.S. in Computer Science awarded from SUNY Buffalo in Buffalo, New York.

Dave is also an active contributor to the open-source community, and the author of several popular libraries (>20,000 monthly downloads) that he continues to manage.
